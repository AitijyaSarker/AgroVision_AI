# 🎤 Agro Vision - Hackathon Demo Script

## 🎯 2-Minute Pitch Structure

### Opening (15 seconds)
> "Agro Vision is an AI-powered platform that helps farmers in Bangladesh detect crop diseases instantly using just a photo. We're solving a critical problem: farmers lose 20-40% of their crops to diseases, and most don't have access to agricultural experts."

### Problem (20 seconds)
> "In Bangladesh, 70% of the population depends on agriculture, but farmers struggle with:
> - No access to disease diagnosis
> - Language barriers (most tools are in English)
> - Limited internet connectivity
> - High cost of agricultural consultation"

### Solution (30 seconds)
> "Agro Vision provides:
> 1. **Instant AI Detection** - Upload a photo, get disease diagnosis in seconds
> 2. **Bilingual Support** - Full Bangla and English interface
> 3. **Expert Connection** - Direct chat with agricultural specialists
> 4. **Mobile-First** - Works on basic smartphones
> 5. **Offline Capable** - Can work with limited connectivity"

### Demo Flow (60 seconds)

#### 1. Show Homepage (5s)
- "Beautiful, modern interface with bilingual support"
- Toggle language: "See how it switches to Bangla instantly"
- Toggle dark mode: "Mobile-friendly dark mode for field use"

#### 2. Show Crop Scan (20s)
- "Let me show you the core feature - disease detection"
- Upload a rice leaf image
- "Our AI analyzes the image..."
- Show results: "Brown Spot Disease, 87% confidence"
- "It provides immediate treatment recommendations"

#### 3. Show AI Chatbot (10s)
- "Farmers can ask questions in Bangla"
- Type: "এই রোগের চিকিৎসা কী?" (What's the treatment for this disease?)
- Show bilingual response

#### 4. Show Specialist Connection (15s)
- "If farmers need expert help, they can request a specialist"
- Show notification system
- "Specialists get real-time notifications and can chat directly"

#### 5. Show Maps (10s)
- "Find nearest agricultural offices"
- Show interactive map with office locations

### Impact (15 seconds)
> "We've built this for real farmers. It's:
> - **Accessible** - Works on any smartphone
> - **Fast** - Results in seconds, not days
> - **Affordable** - Free for farmers
> - **Scalable** - Can serve millions of farmers"

### Closing (10 seconds)
> "Agro Vision is ready to deploy. We have the infrastructure, the AI model is trained, and we're ready to help farmers across Bangladesh. Thank you!"

## 🎯 Q&A Preparation

### "How accurate is your AI?"
> "We achieved 87% accuracy on our test set. We're continuously improving by training on more diverse datasets and fine-tuning the model."

### "What if farmers don't have internet?"
> "The app works offline for viewing results. We're also developing an offline mode where farmers can download the model locally."

### "How do you ensure data privacy?"
> "All images are processed securely. We use Supabase with Row Level Security. Farmers' data is encrypted and never shared."

### "What's your business model?"
> "We're focused on impact first. Potential revenue streams include partnerships with agricultural organizations, government contracts, and premium features for commercial farms."

### "How do you handle different crops?"
> "Our model is trained on multiple crop types. We're expanding to cover all major crops in Bangladesh, starting with rice, wheat, and vegetables."

### "What about specialists - are they real?"
> "Yes, we partner with agricultural universities and extension services. Specialists are verified professionals who volunteer their time."
